About Us

Welcome to Barangay Kanluran! We are a vibrant and close-knit community dedicated to creating a safe, supportive, and inclusive environment for all our residents. Our barangay is committed to transparency, service, and growth, working hand-in-hand with the community to provide essential services, promote welfare, and foster a sense of unity.
At Barangay Kanluran, we strive to improve the quality of life for every resident by delivering responsive public service, maintaining peace and order, and ensuring sustainable development. Through our various programs and initiatives, we aim to create opportunities that empower our community to thrive.

Our Services

We offer a wide range of services to support our residents, including:

Health and Wellness Programs: Regular medical missions, vaccination drives, and health seminars.

Educational Support: Scholarships, after-school programs, and youth training workshops.

Community Safety: Regular patrols, disaster preparedness training, and emergency response services.

Social and Recreational Activities: Events, sports programs, and community outreach initiatives to foster camaraderie.



Barangay Kanluran is more than just a locality; it's a home where every resident is valued and included. Together, let's continue to build a stronger, more resilient, and prosperous barangay.
